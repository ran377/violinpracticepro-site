Frequently Asked Questions

常见问题

What does Violin Practice Pro do?

Violin Practice Pro 是做什么的？

It is an AI-assisted violin practice app that helps users review their practice in a more structured way by combining score files with audio or video performances.

它是一款 AI 辅助的小提琴练习应用，帮助用户通过原谱与演奏录音/录像获得更有结构的练习反馈。

Do I need to upload both a score and a performance?

是否必须同时上传谱子和演奏？

In many cases, uploading both helps the app organize feedback more clearly. Some demo or simplified flows may also be available depending on product version.

通常情况下，同时上传原谱和演奏文件会让反馈组织得更清晰。根据版本不同，也可能提供示例模式或简化流程。

What file types are supported?

支持哪些文件格式？

Support may vary by version, but the app may support score uploads such as MusicXML, PDF, PNG, or JPG, and performance uploads such as audio or video files.

支持格式会根据版本有所不同，但通常可能支持 MusicXML、PDF、PNG、JPG 等谱子格式，以及音频或视频格式的演奏文件。

Is the analysis always exact?

分析结果是否始终精确？

No. The app provides AI-assisted educational guidance and practice support. Results should be used as learning support rather than as a perfect substitute for a live violin teacher.

不是。应用提供的是 AI 辅助的教育与练习支持，结果应作为学习辅助使用，而不是对真人小提琴教师的完全替代。

Can I use the app without a teacher?

可以在没有老师的情况下使用吗？

Yes, the app can support self-practice. However, for important long-term technique development, users may still benefit from learning with a qualified teacher.

可以，本应用适合自主练习使用。但对于长期技术发展和重要学习阶段，仍建议结合专业教师指导。
